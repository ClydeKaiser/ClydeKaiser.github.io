Why does Los Angeles have such a severe traffic issue?

    It mainly comes down to a few things:
  1. There are not enough freeways and not good enough alternatives
  2. Newer western cities like Los Angeles have less developed public transportation in comparision to states like New York, encouraging to use of cars, clogging roads
  3. Some freeways in Los Angeles don't have true intersections and cause clogs on freeway exits
  4. Due to the shear size of the city and car culture in Los Angeles, buying cars is promoted
